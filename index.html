Basics of Hosting a website

Hosting a website is very easy with many options available to a person looking to host their website.

The pricing differs depending on the services you get and there are ways to even host your website for free!

What does a host do?

A webhost provides computing resources used in displaying your web pages to the internet.

They will also provide you with a public IP address for your website and may even provide domain names.

Some hosts may also provide easy design interfaces and web management panels to make it easy to change the content of your website. A more "hands-off" approach can be taken as well if the host deals with updating your website software to make things easier.

So Why Can't I host this Myself?

You can! However, there are many things to consider when going this route. For example most residential internet providers do not give you a static IP which could change and result in making your web pages not visible. There are ways around this with different DNS services. Another consideration is the reliability of your internet uptime. If your internet goes out during a snow storm no one can visit your page!

Considerations

There are many questions you have to ask yourself when choosing a host for your website. Is this a personal website or business? Does it vital for services and need to be available all the time? How much am I willing to pay monthly? Will I be hosting this for a short time or for as long as I can imagine?
